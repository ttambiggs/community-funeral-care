About Us

At Community Funeral Care, we believe that every farewell should be as unique as the life it honours. Founded by Laurie, our family-owned business has been built on a foundation of compassion, trust, and genuine care for the families we serve.

Laurie brings decades of personal experience and a deep commitment to walking alongside families during one of life's most difficult moments — from the first phone call to the final farewell.

We are available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, because grief doesn't keep business hours.
